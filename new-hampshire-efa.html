What families receive with Literacy Arcade Plus Family

Literacy Arcade Plus Family is a one-year digital subscription for children in grades K–6 — including beginning readers, struggling readers, and children with dyslexia. It combines reading assessments, fluency tools, customizable practice, and guided instructional support across the five essential areas of reading: phonemic awareness, phonics, fluency, vocabulary, and comprehension.

Use built-in content or add your own words, sentences, vocabulary, and comprehension questions to support your curriculum, intervention plan, or reading goals. Literacy Arcade is supplemental — it strengthens a core curriculum rather than replacing it.

Included with Plus Family

Monthly guidance for reading at home

Families can ask a literacy specialist about assessment results, what to teach next, and how to use Literacy Arcade tools to support their child's reading needs.

Included through a monthly live group literacy Q&A with Chelsea Herndon Warner, Ph.D., a literacy specialist and the creator of Literacy Arcade.

New Hampshire EFA questions

Is Literacy Arcade approved for New Hampshire EFA?

Yes. Literacy Arcade is an approved Education Service Provider for the New Hampshire Education Freedom Account program.

What does Plus Family include?

The full Literacy Arcade Plus platform for children in one household, plus monthly guidance for reading at home through a live group literacy Q&A with a literacy specialist.

Is Literacy Arcade Plus also available?

Yes. Plus includes the full digital platform. It does not include the monthly live group literacy Q&A, which is part of Plus Family.

What is the difference between Plus and Plus Family?

Both include the full platform. Plus Family adds monthly guidance for reading at home through the live group literacy Q&A.

Is Literacy Arcade a complete curriculum?

No. It is supplemental — it strengthens a family's core curriculum or intervention program with assessment, targeted instruction, and reading practice.

What grades does Literacy Arcade support?

Literacy Arcade primarily supports literacy learning in grades K–6.

Do children need accounts?

No. Children can use shared activities without creating Literacy Arcade accounts or providing student email addresses.

Can I purchase directly without using EFA funds?

Yes. Families not using EFA funds may purchase a direct Literacy Arcade Plus plan on the Plus pricing page.
