Questions educators and families often ask

A few quick details before you choose a plan.

Do students need accounts?

No. Student-ready activities can be opened without student accounts.

How can I share activities with students?

Plus members can share locked student-ready activities through Google Classroom or direct links. Students do not need Literacy Arcade accounts.

Can I keep using the free tools?

Yes. Free accounts can still use available Literacy Arcade tools live and create up to five saved or shared activities or tools. Complete assessment analysis and printable reports require a paid plan.

Does standard Plus include monthly Q&A?

No. The monthly live group literacy Q&A with a reading specialist is included only with the $99 Plus Family Annual subscription.

What is Plus Family?

Plus Family is the annual homeschool plan. It includes all standard Plus features for children in one household, plus a monthly live group literacy Q&A with a reading specialist.

Can I use Plus Family with more than one child?

Yes. Plus Family may be used with children in one household. Children do not need individual Literacy Arcade accounts.

Is the Q&A private tutoring?

No. The monthly Q&A is a live group educational session. It does not include private tutoring, therapy, diagnosis, formal evaluation, or individualized special education services.

What happens after checkout?

Stripe securely handles direct website checkout. After payment is confirmed, the subscription should update the signed-in Literacy Arcade account automatically.

Which email should I use?

Sign in with the Literacy Arcade account you want upgraded before direct checkout. For marketplace purchases, follow the activation instructions supplied after the order.

Can I cancel the monthly plan?

Yes. Standard Plus Monthly can be canceled before the next billing cycle. Billing is managed through Stripe.

What happens to activities I already saved?

Existing saved and shared activities remain available. Free-account saving limits apply when the account does not have an active paid plan.

What if I want access for a school or team?

Email hello@literacyarcade.com for group or school pricing.
