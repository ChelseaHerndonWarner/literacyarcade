How It Works

Choose how you want to use Literacy Arcade

Open a quick game, customize word-based practice, build a teacher-led letterbox lesson, or save selected activities for later.

1

Play a ready-to-use game

Start with ready-to-play games like Spellbound and Word Wizard Quest for quick phonics, spelling, and word-solving practice. More ready-to-play word study games are coming soon.

2

Customize word-based practice

Use customizable tools when you want to choose the exact words, skills, or practice format.

3

Build and teach a letterbox lesson

Start in the Phoneme Counter to choose words, map graphemes to phonemes in Elkonin boxes, and launch a teacher-led Letterbox Lesson.

4

Save and share activities

Save supported activities, reopen them later, and share student-ready links to Google Classroom.

Looking for Letterbox Lessons?

Start with the Phoneme Counter. It helps you choose or enter words, count phonemes, map graphemes to phonemes in Elkonin boxes, and then open the Letterbox Lesson for teacher-led instruction.
