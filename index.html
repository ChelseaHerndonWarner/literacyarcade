Simple Tools for Phonics and Fluency Practice

Literacy Arcade gives teachers, tutors, interventionists, and families fast digital tools for structured literacy instruction, reading fluency practice, word reading review, and progress monitoring.

For word reading practice

Use built-in or custom word lists for phonics patterns, sight words, Fry words, Dolch words, vocabulary, and decodable word review.

For oral reading fluency

Track WCPM, accuracy, repeated reading growth, and oral reading fluency data using teacher-friendly fluency tools.

For flexible instruction

Use Literacy Arcade for whole-group instruction, whiteboards, small groups, tutoring, intervention, centers, and at-home practice.
